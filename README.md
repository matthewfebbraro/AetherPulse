# Aether Pulse

**AetherPulse.one** is the central hub for my projects, portfolio, and creative work. It serves as both a showcase and a gateway — connecting visitors to apps, experiments, and concepts in development.

## Features

- **Project Showcase** – Highlighting completed and in-progress builds.
- **Portfolio Access** – Direct links to repositories and live demos.
- **About Section** – Background, skills, and development journey.
- **Contact & Links** – Ways to connect or collaborate.

## Purpose

Aether Pulse is designed to present my work in a clear, engaging way — whether for potential collaborators, recruiters, or anyone curious about the things I’m building.
